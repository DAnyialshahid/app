About Us

Who are We ? What CouponChaska.com is all about ?

CouponChaska.com is all about Coupons, offers and deals listing website of Indian online shopping sites. CouponChaska has partnered with more that 1000+ popular online stores like Flipkart, Amazon.in, Snapdeal, Paytm, jabong, Paytm, ebay.in, yatra.com, makemytrip, Dominos, Freecharge and many more Stores. CouponChaska brings exciting coupons and deals on wide range of products from retailers from all domains such as fashion, electronics, travel, food, Recharges, gifts and flowers etc, which enable you to save extra money on all online purchases apart from the discount offered by the online retailer/stores.

How did we get started?

With the surge in Indian online marketing there comes the thousands of stores in the market and with this the tough competition to target the customers. To attract the user to its store they started offering huge discount and coupons. With 1000 of stores offering discount and coupon code online all across internet, It leads to confusion among shoppers in choosing best product with best offer. So we decided to help shopper to choose the best offer available across the stores all at one place. CouponChaska was founded with the aim to bridge the gap between online shopping sites and shopper.

Our Vision

Our vision is to make online shopping a good experience with satisfaction. An experience that every shopper wants to re-experience.
